Our Story

The first dedicated sober
social venue in the Twin Cities.

Howl & Bloom is a first-of-its-kind destination — a warm, craft coffee house by day and a sophisticated, fully alcohol-free social lounge by evening. The name nods to its inspiration: Howl at the Moon, a pioneering sober bar in Thunder Bay, Ontario, and the idea of blooming into a fuller, more intentional life.

Hopkins is the ideal home — a walkable, community-minded Twin Cities neighborhood with the local character and foot traffic that makes a genuine third place thrive.

What Makes Us Different
